## centralized exchange (CEX):
centralized exchange is where you can buy, trade crypto currency using fiat money (traditional money).

1. [Binance](https://www.binance.com/en)
2. [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/)
3. [CoinMarketCap](https://coinmarketcap.com/)
4. [coingecko](https://www.coingecko.com/en)
5. [gate.io](https://www.gate.io/)

## hardware wallet(cool wallet):
hardware wallet is the wallet to keep your cryptocurrency (BTC, Eth,..) without worry of losing your crypto currency as long as you remember the seed phrase, or secret phrase.
If you plan to keep your cryptocurrency forever, this wallet is a must to have. hardware wallet is offline to make sure your wallet never get hacked by hacker and it also add another layer to protect you from hacker by ask you to approve in every transaction.

1. [Ledger](https://www.ledger.com/)
2. [Trezor](https://trezor.io/)
3. [Safepal](https://safepal.io/)

## software cryptocurrency wallet:
To used any DAPP you may need to have software cryptocurrency wallet, It comes with form of browser extension or addon.

1. [Metamask](https://metamask.io/)
2. [C98](https://docs.coin98.com/products/coin98-wallet/extension)
3. [Terra station - terra luna chain](https://docs.terra.money/quickstart.html#download-terra-station)
4. [Phantom - solana chain](https://phantom.app/)
